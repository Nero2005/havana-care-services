Rehabilitation Services

Yanicare offers a range of specialist rehabilitation services to people with learning disabilities, autism with complex needs such as mental health problems and challenging behaviours.

All our services are specifically designed for individuals who would benefit from receiving direct support in single placement (or up to a maximum of 3) accommodation suited for the development of daily living skills.

We offer person centred and care planned outcomes focused interventions to support recovery to better health and ultimately providing a greater sense of self-determination. We assist our service users with engagement in the community and provide the opportunity to develop new lifestyle choices to improve selfesteem and confidence and ensure people are living meaningful lives.

We believe that the choice of the service user is central to everything we do, and we make it a point of duty to ensure that we respond to service users changing needs, reviewing and revising plans to meet these needs. We understand that there is much value positive risk-taking opportunities and facilitate this within the framework of robust risk assessment and a clear management strategy.

Our services are staffed by an experienced team of professional managers and highly experienced support workers. We have ongoing access to psychiatrists, psychologists and assistants, occupational therapists, speech and language therapists and advocacy.

Our services are well-led by caring, professional and experienced staff and our senior management team is always close to service users, staff teams and service managers, so our care is developed in direct response to peopleâ€™s needs.

We continually measure the effectiveness of our interventions and regularly seek feedback from the people we support, our staff, and case managers and commissioners. We are dedicated to monitoring our service delivery in order to ensure that we are able to improve services whilst simultaneously meeting changing demands.

Our approach to Person-Centred care:

  • Use of person-centred care planning
  • A tailored recovery and rehabilitation support for each individual
  • A clear strategic approach to enable positive risk taking
  • A focus on using Strengths based methods and STAR recovery models
  • Positive behaviour management strategies
  • Access to daily expressive and motivating activities
  • The progression of independent living skills in the home setting and within the community setting
  • The continual monitoring and reviewing of safeguarding
  • Rigorous clinical governance maintained
  • Identifying the rights of the service user to access independent advocacy
  • Partnering with family and professionals creating a holistic package of care and pathway

Our Service Outcomes

At Yanicare we aim to deliver the following outcomes:

  • Support to give the service user to become confident in making positive choices, having control and independence
  • Promoting social inclusion by encouraging service users to join in with appropriate activities in the community
  • The maintenance and sustaining of good physical health through diet and exercise
  • Improved self-esteem and greater feelings of self- worth of the service user
  • The service user feeling valued through being treated with dignity and respect
  • Successful transition into a semi-independent living, if appropriate
